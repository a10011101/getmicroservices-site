PostAutobot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

Contact for Questions

1. Introduction

PostAutobot ("we", "our", or "us") is a LinkedIn automation tool developed by GetMicroservices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our PostAutobot service.

By using PostAutobot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 LinkedIn Account Information

When you connect PostAutobot to your LinkedIn account, we collect:

  • Your LinkedIn profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • LinkedIn OAuth2 access tokens (for posting on your behalf)
  • Your LinkedIn posts and engagement data (only when you use our service)

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use PostAutobot:

  • Post scheduling preferences
  • Content you submit for posting
  • API request logs
  • Error reports and diagnostics

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Timestamps of API requests

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: To post content to LinkedIn on your behalf
  • Scheduling: To manage and execute scheduled posts
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Communication: To send service notifications and updates
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

  • LinkedIn access tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • Post content is stored only until successfully posted to LinkedIn
  • Logs are retained for 30 days for debugging purposes

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure deletion of data upon request

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

With trusted third-party services that help us operate PostAutobot:

  • Cloud hosting providers (for infrastructure)
  • Monitoring and analytics services
  • Customer support platforms

5.2 Legal Requirements

When required by law or to protect our rights:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect against legal liability
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing

5.3 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition.

6. LinkedIn API Compliance

PostAutobot complies with LinkedIn's API Terms of Service:

  • We only access data necessary for posting functionality
  • We respect LinkedIn's rate limits and usage guidelines
  • We do not store LinkedIn passwords
  • We use OAuth2 for secure authentication

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

7.2 Account Management

  • Revoke PostAutobot's access to your LinkedIn account at any time
  • Delete your PostAutobot account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at: privacy@getmicroservices.com
