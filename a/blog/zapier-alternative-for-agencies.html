The Best Zapier Alternative for Agencies in 2026

Agencies hit the Zapier wall fast. 100,000 tasks/month on Zapier costs $599. The same volume on n8n costs $12 (your VPS). Here's an honest breakdown of your options.

Why Agencies Hit the Zapier Wall

Every action in a multi-step Zap counts as a separate task. A 5-step workflow triggered 500 times = 2,500 tasks. At agency scale, task limits burn fast. 100,000 tasks/month on Zapier: $599.

n8n: The Main Alternative

Open-source, self-hosted, unlimited tasks. Runs on a $12/month VPS. No per-task pricing, no vendor lock-in.

  • Unlimited tasks at fixed infrastructure cost
  • Full code execution (JS/Python nodes)
  • Data stays on your infrastructure
  • Custom nodes for any API

Trade-offs: ~400 native integrations vs Zapier's 7,000+. Technical setup required. You manage updates and uptime.

Make: The Middle Ground

Cloud-hosted like Zapier, more powerful, better pricing. 100k operations/month: $99. Best for agencies that want more power without managing infrastructure.
