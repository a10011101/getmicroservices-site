1. Introduction

PostAutobot is a LinkedIn automation tool that helps users schedule and manage posts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 LinkedIn Data

  • LinkedIn profile information (via OAuth2)
  • Access tokens for posting on your behalf
  • Posts you schedule through our service

2.2 Usage Data

  • Scheduling preferences
  • API request logs
  • Error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To post content to LinkedIn as scheduled
  • To improve our service
  • For security and fraud prevention
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure storage of access tokens
  • Regular security audits
  • Data deletion upon request

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service providers necessary for operation
  • When required by law

6. LinkedIn API Compliance

  • Uses official LinkedIn API
  • Respects rate limits and terms
  • OAuth2 authentication only
  • No password storage

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Revoke LinkedIn access anytime

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while active
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 12 months
  • Logs: Retained for 30 days
