Built For These Teams

Customer Support Teams

Handle 60–80% of incoming tickets automatically. AI reads the query, pulls context, and either resolves it or routes to the right person with a summary.

Ops Managers in Email Hell

AI reads every email, classifies it, flags urgent items to Slack, auto-forwards to the right teammate, and sends a daily digest. 15 minutes of email a day.

Finance Teams Processing Invoices

AI extracts line items, PO numbers, and amounts from PDFs, validates against your records, and flags discrepancies — without anyone touching a spreadsheet.

Agencies Qualifying Leads

AI bot asks the right questions, scores the lead, books a call if qualified, or sends a polite no. Your team only talks to people worth talking to.
