founder note

No Agency Theatre

We've seen it too many times: a business owner with a clear, solvable problem sits through weeks of "discovery," signs off on a 40-page SOW, and ends up paying $40–80k for something that could've been built in 5 days by two developers who actually gave a damn.

That's not us.

GetMicroservices started because we wanted to work differently — directly with the people who have the problem, not the account managers who translate them into tickets. We keep the team small on purpose. Small means fast. Small means you're talking to the person who writes the code.

We've shipped automation workflows, Telegram bots, booking systems, lead pipelines, data scrapers, and a dozen other things you'd normally wait months for. Most projects ship in under a week. All of them come with source code you actually own.

The deal is simple: you tell us what's eating your time or holding back your revenue. We fix it, fast, at a price that makes sense.
